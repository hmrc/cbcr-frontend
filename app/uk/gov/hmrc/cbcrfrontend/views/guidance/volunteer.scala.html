If you aren't required to send HMRC a report, your multinational group may still send one voluntarily under certain circumstances.

Register and report using the country-by-country reporting service.

Overview

You can send a country-by-country report if your group parent business isn’t based in the UK but you have a UK group member that isn’t required to file a country-by-country report.

Your group parent business must be in a country which meets one of the following conditions:

• it isn’t required to send a country-by-country report
• it has entered into an international agreement allowing information to be exchanged, and either:
    • there are no specific arrangements to exchange country-by-country reports
    • HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) has notified your group parent business those arrangements aren’t working effectively

Your group parent business must give you permission to send a voluntary country-by-country report. They’ll need to notify HMRC in writing that you have their permission, which should be sent to notification.cbcrfiling@@hmrc.gsi.gov.uk.

Register to report

Once you’ve been given permission by your group parent business to send a country-by-country report, you’ll need to register.

Send your report

Once you’ve registered, you can send your report.

Further information

You can find more details on the above in the International Exchange of Information Manual.
