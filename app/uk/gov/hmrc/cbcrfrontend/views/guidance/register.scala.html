Register to use the country-by-country reporting service.

Overview

If you need to send HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) a report, you must first register to use the service.

Who must register

You’ll need to register if your multinational group as a whole has a consolidated revenue of at least €750 million.

There are other requirements in relation to who must register.

If you want to send a voluntary report to HMRC, you’ll also have to register.

Your details

Before you register, you’ll need an HMRC unique taxpayer reference (UTR) for one of your group’s businesses, and the postcode for your UK registered office that matches that UTR.

In addition, you’ll need the person HMRC should contact in relation to your registration, including their:

  • full name
  • phone number
  • email address

How to register

Once you have this information, you’ll need to sign in to the registration service by using your Government Gateway account.

You’ll receive a country-by-country ID (CbCID) number at the end of the registration.

You must make a note of your CbCID number as you’ll need it in order to complete and send your report.

Appoint an agent to report for you

You can use an agent to complete and send your reports to HMRC by giving them your CbCID number.

Your agent will need to sign in through their own Government Gateway account in order to use your CbCID number.

Further information

You can find more details on the above in the International Exchange of Information Manual.
